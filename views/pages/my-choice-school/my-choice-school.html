Education and Support

My Choice is committed to increasing access to education and supporting and promoting active participation in education for all young people.

Where any young person is unable to access a local school, for whatever reason, they may be registered at My Choice School. Students may spend their time with us as a transition programme while awaiting alternative school provision or may spend their time with us re-engaging with learning and preparing for their future.

My Choice School provides a teacher and a resourced classroom within each home. Teachers at My Choice School are experienced in working with challenging and complex behaviours and work with flexibility and enthusiasm while providing boundaries, structure and opportunity to succeed. RSWs provide support to the student in the classroom and on activities.

Annie Murphy Head Teacher

Annie has a BSc (hons) in Experimental Psychology and a PGCE from the University of Sussex. Annie has 25 years experience of managing services for children and young people in residential, play, training and education settings; with 11years as the Headteacher of My Choice School. Annie is responsible for the management of all My Choice Schools, including management of teaching staff, writing school policies and development plans, ensuring standards are maintained and raised in line with DfE and Ofsted regulations, monitoring student performance and progress, monitoring IEPs and writing reports for PEPs, EHCP Annual Reviews and LAC reviews.
