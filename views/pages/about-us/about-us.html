About My Choice Homes

Our Homes

Our homes provide a comfortable, non-institutional environment for young people to begin to work towards their future and independence, or return to home or to alternative carers. We are committed to and support the implementation of Care Plans for short, medium and long term placements.

Our Tools

We have developed many tools such as our own Placement Plans (in line with Standard 2 of the Minimum Standards for Children's Homes), which enables us to work closely with the young people to encourage and devise appropriate Care Plans identifying their current and future needs.

National Care Standards

Since the implementation of the National Care Standards in April 2002, My Choice has established a very positive relationship with the Commission. We work in partnership with all our Placing Authorities believing open and constant communication between the Home, Young Person, Parents, Families and the Social Worker is essential to ensure effective and safe childcare.

Supportive Framework

Every young person brings with them unique life experiences and as such we treat all as individuals, being very clear about their specific needs.

We look after the young person, not the perceived problem.

Our service seeks to provide an effective and supportive framework for Young People who would otherwise be hard to place.

Every member of our team, whether RSW, Care Manager, Maintenance Worker, Office worker or Director, wants to be the best. We work hard to attract and retain truly talented people. People who share the same vision and approach, who help our young people to be the best that they can.

We believe that our people underpin our strategic direction, provide a great energy, approachability and service excellence. Here at My Choice we embrace a culture where people can flourish and develop through hard work and commitment.
