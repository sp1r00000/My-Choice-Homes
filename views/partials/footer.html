My Choice Children's Homes

We have 8 Children's Homes located in between West / East Sussex and Surrey and we look after children who display challenging behaviours aged between 10 - 18 years of age.

My Choice Children's Homes also provide extensive training to help support our staff meet the challenging needs of our children plus we provide Diploma Qualifications via our in-house Diploma Assessor.
